Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Eggs (large) 2
Pancetta (or bacon) 100g
Parmesan cheese 50g, grated
Garlic clove 1, minced
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Step 1:Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (about 8-10 minutes). Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2:While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until crispy and golden brown. Add the minced garlic to the pan and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant. Remove from heat.

Step 3:In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan, salt, and black pepper until smooth and well combined.

Step 4:Once the pasta is drained, add it directly to the pan with the pancetta and garlic. Toss the pasta to coat in the rendered fat and garlic.

Step 5:Slowly pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water, a little at a time, until you reach your desired consistency.

Step 6:Divide the carbonara between plates, top with extra grated Parmesan and freshly ground black pepper. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast (boneless, skinless) 2 breasts, sliced
Fettuccine pasta 200g
Heavy cream 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 1 cup, grated
Garlic clove 2, minced
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Step 1:Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (about 10-12 minutes). Drain and set aside, reserving about 1/2 cup of pasta water.

Step 2:In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced chicken breast, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 6-8 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

Step 5:Add the cooked fettuccine and chicken back into the pan with the sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta and chicken are well coated in the creamy sauce.

Step 6:Divide the pasta between plates, sprinkle with fresh parsley (if desired), and serve immediately. Enjoy!
